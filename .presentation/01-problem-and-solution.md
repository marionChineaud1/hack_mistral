# 1. Problem and solution

**Time:** 0:00–0:15, immediately after starting the live demo

## Slide content

### Viral claims spread before their context

- People see claims before they see the source, chronology, evidence, or limitations.
- A binary “true or false” answer hides uncertainty and teaches users little about how a claim was constructed.

### Vera turns a post into an investigation

- traces origin and chronology;
- consults live web, news, and institutional sources;
- identifies missing context and framing;
- saves a shareable evidence report.

## Speaker notes

“While Vera starts investigating this post, here is the problem we are solving. Viral information often reaches us before the context needed to interpret it. A simple true-or-false label is not enough: it hides the quality of sources, what is missing, and what is still uncertain.

Vera turns an X post into a transparent investigation. It makes the work visible, then saves the evidence trail so people can inspect it instead of trusting a chat answer.”
