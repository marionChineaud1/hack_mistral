# 9. Demo video script

**Target duration:** 90 seconds  
**Delivery:** Confident, clear, and conversational. Leave short pauses while the interface changes.

## 0:00–0:06 — The problem

**Show:** Vera title card, then Mistral Vibe with an X post URL ready.

**Say:**

> Information moves at feed speed. Verification does not.
>
> Every day, people see viral claims without the source, context, or evidence needed to understand them.

## 0:06–0:14 — The product

**Show:** Paste the X post URL and begin the investigation.

**Say:**

> Vera is the investigation layer for online information.
>
> In Mistral Vibe, anyone can investigate a claim by sharing one link. No technical setup, tool configuration, or prompt-engineering expertise is required.

## 0:14–0:28 — The live investigation

**Show:** Visible investigation statuses and MCP activity.

**Say:**

> Vera turns that post into a live investigation. It traces the original source, checks web and institutional evidence, finds missing context, and analyses how the message is framed.
>
> These statuses are not just a loading screen. They show the work behind the conclusion.

## 0:28–0:40 — The connection

**Show:** Architecture diagram.

**Say:**

> Mistral Vibe is the interface. The Vera Skill is the investigative playbook. It orchestrates MCP tools that retrieve and analyse live information.
>
> Then, once the evidence is synthesised, Vera creates a structured investigation, rather than leaving the result inside a chat.

## 0:40–0:48 — The durable evidence trail

**Show:** `save_investigation` or *Enregistrement de l’enquête détaillée*.

**Say:**

> The report is saved with its sources, evidence, limitations, and citations. That makes the result shareable, inspectable, and useful after the conversation ends.

## 0:48–0:58 — The concise result

**Show:** Final Mistral Vibe response. Click **Consulter l’enquête complète**.

**Say:**

> Vibe stays concise: a cautious conclusion, one decisive source, and one link to the full report.
>
> The detail belongs in the evidence trail, not in a wall of chat text.

## 0:58–1:15 — The public report

**Show:** Claim under review, quick conclusion, explanation, and key limitation.

**Say:**

> Here, Vera separates the claim from the evidence. In this case, the statement is partly supported, but it leaves out important context about heat-related mortality.
>
> It makes both the finding and its limits visible.

## 1:15–1:24 — Trust through transparency

**Show:** Sources, supporting and qualifying evidence, then rhetoric analysis.

**Say:**

> Readers can inspect the primary and secondary sources, what supports the claim, what qualifies it, what remains uncertain, and how the wording shapes the message.

## 1:24–1:30 — The vision

**Show:** *What could change this reading*, then the Vera brand.

**Say:**

> Today, Vera starts with X posts. Next, the same investigation pipeline expands to web pages, PDFs, images, and video.
>
> Before information earns attention, it should earn investigation.

## One-take narration

> Information moves at feed speed. Verification does not. Every day, people see viral claims without the source, context, or evidence needed to understand them. Vera is the investigation layer for online information. In Mistral Vibe, anyone can investigate a claim by sharing one link, with no technical setup or prompt-engineering expertise. Vera turns that post into a live investigation: it traces the original source, checks web and institutional evidence, finds missing context, and analyses framing. Mistral Vibe is the interface, while the Vera Skill orchestrates MCP tools that retrieve and analyse live information. Once the evidence is synthesised, Vera saves a structured report with sources, limitations, and citations. Vibe gives the user a concise conclusion and a link to the complete report. There, readers can inspect what supports the claim, what qualifies it, what remains uncertain, and what could change the conclusion. Today, Vera starts with X posts. Next, the same pipeline expands to web pages, PDFs, images, and video. Before information earns attention, it should earn investigation.
