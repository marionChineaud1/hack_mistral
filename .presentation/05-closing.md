# 5. Closing

**Time:** 15 seconds

## Slide content

> Most AI assistants give an answer. North shows the work required to deserve one.

- Evidence, not authority.
- Uncertainty, not false certainty.
- A durable report, not an opaque chat response.

## Speaker notes

“North does not replace journalists or professional fact-checkers. It gives people a transparent starting point: what we know, what remains uncertain, and what evidence could change the conclusion.

Every viral post deserves an investigation before it deserves your attention.”

## Likely jury questions

### Is North deciding the truth?

“No. North presents evidence quality, contradictory findings, limitations, and missing context. Its conclusion is cautious and revisable.”

### Why does rhetoric analysis matter?

“Factual accuracy and fair presentation are different questions. A claim can be partly factual while still being framed in a misleadingly certain or incomplete way.”

### Why save the report?

“Saving structured citations and uncertainty makes the result inspectable, shareable, and durable beyond a single chat session.”
